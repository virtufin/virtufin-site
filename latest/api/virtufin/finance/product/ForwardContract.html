virtufin.finance.product

ForwardContract

case class ForwardContract[A <: Instrument](assetPosition: Position[A], payment: Cash, settlementDate: Time, buyerPortfolio: PortfolioIdentifier, sellerPortfolio: PortfolioIdentifier) extends Instrument with BilateralContract with SingleSettling with FixedPositionTransfers with Product with Serializable

Exchange at maturity date a asset position with a cash position, i.e. buy an asset at maturity at a pre-determined price.

A

type of the asset

assetPosition

the asset position being bought/sold. A positive amount means buying, a negative selling.

payment

the amount being paid. The amount is negative for buying an asset, and positive for selling an asset.

settlementDate
buyerPortfolio

portfolio of the party, which buys the Asset and sells the Cash

sellerPortfolio

portfolio of the party, which sells the Asset and buys the Cash
